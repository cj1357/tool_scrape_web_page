How to build and send a proposal in Houzz Pro

Open your Houzz Pro dashboard and tap New Project. Choose a room template or import your 2D floor sketch. Drag real products from the built-in catalog to populate the board, the AI Clipper grabs vendor info and prices in seconds. Hit Pricing, adjust margins, and the software auto-totals labor, tax, and shipping. Click Preview Proposal to see a clean client-ready PDF. Add your cover note, press Send, and the file lands in the client's inbox branded with your logo.

Wow Clients With AR Measurements and Photorealistic Moodboards

Imagine a client slipping on AR glasses and seeing their future living room life-size right in front of them. No tape measures, no guessing games, just instant wow. That is what today’s AR room measurement tools deliver, and designers who use them turn hesitant prospects into excited partners on the spot.

Built-in AR features replace the old measuring dance. Apps like ARPLAN 3D let you point your phone at a wall and capture exact dimensions in seconds. Meanwhile, Morpholio Board’s AR Colour Capture grabs real paint shades from any surface and drops them straight onto your digital moodboard, so the colors you show are the colors they will get.
