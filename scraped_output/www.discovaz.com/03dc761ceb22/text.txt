How to build and send a proposal in Houzz Pro

Wow Clients With AR Measurements and Photorealistic Moodboards

Imagine a client slipping on AR glasses and seeing their future living room life-size right in front of them. No tape measures, no guessing games, just instant wow. That is what today’s AR room measurement tools deliver, and designers who use them turn hesitant prospects into excited partners on the spot.

Built-in AR features replace the old measuring dance. Apps like ARPLAN 3D let you point your phone at a wall and capture exact dimensions in seconds. The software then spins those numbers into crisp 2D and 3D floor plans you can share before the coffee gets cold. Meanwhile, Morpholio Board’s AR Colour Capture grabs real paint shades from any surface and drops them straight onto your digital moodboard, so the colors you show are the colors they will get.
